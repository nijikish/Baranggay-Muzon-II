Biography

Baranggay Captain

Hener C. Vargas

Hener C. Vargas is the current barangay captain of Brgy. Muzon II in Rosario, Cavite. He became the barangay captain on the 31st of October, 2023. He won the 2023 Barangay Elections with a will in his heart to serve his fellow ka-barangays.

Councilor

Denmark R. Baquiran

Denmark R. Baquiran is one of the councilors of Brgy. Muzon II in Rosario, Cavite. He was elected councilor on the 31st of October, 2023. Denmark R. Baquiran is among the seven councilors who won in the 2023 Barangay Elections, which happened on October 29, 2023.

SK Chairman

Rachel Ann Pulhin

Rachel Ann Pulhin is the current Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) Chairman of Brgy. Muzon II in Rosario, Cavite. She was elected as the SK chairman in October 31, 2023. Rachel Ann Pulhin studied BS Information Technology in Cavite State University - CCAT Campus and graduated on August 2022 as a Cum Laude. On 2023, she ran in the Barangay Elections as SK Chairman. She won the elections due to the platforms she shared to her ka-barangays that will help the youth and all the citizens in their barangay.
